DIY Typing Animation – License & Info

Thank you for installing DIY Typing Animation!
This delightful little companion sits quietly on your screen and animates a chibi that types whenever you type, rests when you stop, and sleeps when you step away. It's meant to bring a bit of charm and whimsy to your everyday work.
By using this app, you agree to the following:
- This application is free to use for personal, non-commercial purposes.
- It does NOT collect, transmit, or store any user data.
- No keystrokes are logged or saved.
- No internet connections are made in the background.
- Nothing you do is ever monitored or sent elsewhere.
- The app runs locally on your system and does not affect any performance, files, or security of your PC.
- You are free to uninstall the application at any time using standard Windows settings or the Start Menu shortcut.
This software is provided “as is,” without warranty of any kind. All rights remain with the original developer: rishi-rj-s.
Let this animated chibi be a quiet friend—not a noisy one. Enjoy the experience and type happily!
